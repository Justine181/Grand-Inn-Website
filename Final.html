Our Story

A Sanctuary of Comfort & Grace

Nestled in the spiritual heart of Tiruvannamalai, Grand Inn is a haven of refined comfort and warm hospitality. We believe that every traveler—whether on a sacred pilgrimage or a peaceful retreat—deserves an extraordinary stay.

Our hotel is thoughtfully designed to blend modern luxury with the rich cultural heritage of Tamil Nadu. From spotlessly clean rooms to personalized service, every detail reflects our commitment to excellence and guest satisfaction.

Just minutes from the magnificent Arunachaleswarar Temple, Grand Inn places you at the spiritual center of Tiruvannamalai—yet in a world of serene comfort away from the bustle of the city.

Culinary Journey

Authentic South Indian Flavors

Begin your day with a traditional South Indian breakfast before your morning temple visit, or return from the sacred Girivalam walk to a comforting thali—our kitchen is always ready to nourish your soul.
